Our Purpose

Mission
& Vision

The Vision

To create a digitally resilient Kerala where every citizen, regardless of age or background, can navigate the internet without fear of exploitation.

The Mission

Educating the public on cyber hygiene, providing real-time scam alerts, and offering accessible tools to identify and report digital threats effectively.

01

Inclusion

Tailored awareness programs specifically for the elderly and women to bridge the digital safety gap.

02

Prevention

Proactive identification of phishing links and fraudulent schemes before they cause harm.

03

Response

Streamlined pathways for citizens to report cybercrimes to the appropriate authorities.
