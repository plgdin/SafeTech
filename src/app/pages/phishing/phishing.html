How to spot Risky URLs

🧐

Domain Spoofing

Attackers use misspellings like 'paypa1.com' to mislead you. SafeTech's engine detects these character manipulations.

🔒

Protocol check

Sensitive portals without HTTPS are extreme risks. SafeTech flags any insecure protocol on financial domains.

🌍

Risky Extensions

Be wary of .zip or .tk extensions. These are often used to hide malware payloads from basic filters.
