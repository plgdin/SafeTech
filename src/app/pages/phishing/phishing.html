Anatomy of a Cyber Attack

SafeTech identifies patterns that the human eye often misses.

🔍

Homograph Attacks

Using look-alike characters (like replacing 'o' with '0') to mimic bank portals. Our Punycode detector isolates these instantly.

🏗️

Subdomain Tunneling

Hiding malicious intent deep within nested subdomains (e.g., login.bank.com.secure-update.tk) to bypass simple filters.

⚡

DGA Detection

Identifying Domain Generation Algorithms that create random-looking URLs to communicate with malware servers.
