How to spot Risky URLs

🧐

Domain Spoofing

Look for subtle misspellings like 'paypa1.com' vs 'paypal.com'. Attackers use look-alike characters to deceive you.

🔒

HTTP vs HTTPS

Secure sites use HTTPS. A lack of encryption on a "login" or "banking" page is a major red flag.

🌍

Risky Extensions

Be wary of links ending in .zip, .xyz, or .top. These are frequently used for hosting malicious payloads.
